📐 The Mathematics

Phase Oracle \( Z_f \)

Marks "good" states by phase flip:

$$ Z_f |x\rangle = (-1)^{f(x)} |x\rangle $$

Where \( f(x) = 1 \) if score\( (x) \geq \theta \)

Diffusion Operator

Reflects across average amplitude:

$$ D|\psi\rangle = 2\langle\psi| s\rangle |s\rangle - |\psi\rangle $$

Where \( |s\rangle \) = uniform superposition

Optimal Iterations

$$ k_\text{opt} \approx \frac{\pi}{4} \sqrt{\frac{N}{M}} $$

Your demo: \( k = 3 \) iterations → 67% probability on best antibody

  • Diffusion: Reflect across average → amplify!
